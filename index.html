Instructions

Welcome to the Treasure Quest...!!


  • You will be given a chance to click one out of all boxes.
  • Each box is having a survival and a danger in it.
  • Choosing survival will lead you to next level and increase your points.
  • Caught by danger will lead you to the end of the game.
  • At the end you will reach to the treasure which will give you maximum points.
  • Lets test your luck with just single click.Good Luck....!!
